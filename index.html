Welcome to the Club!

DC Riichi Mahjong is a vibrant social club dedicated to fostering the appreciation and enjoyment of Riichi Mahjong, a popular strategic tile-based game hailing from Japan.

Known for our friendly atmosphere and diverse community, our members range from beginners to experienced players, all brought together by their shared love of this captivating game.

Play Mahjong Every Sunday!

We host a free Riichi Mahjong meet up every Sunday in McLean, Virginia from 12pm - 6pm. We provide all of the equipment to play, but you are more than welcome to bring your own if you have it. If you have never played before, we offer teaching sessions that will take you from zero to hero in just a few hours.

Location: Whole Foods Market
1635 Boro Pl, McLean, VA 22102

There are several $5 all day parking garages within 1 block of the Whole Foods. It is also just a 5 minute walk from the Greensboro Silver Line Metro stop.
